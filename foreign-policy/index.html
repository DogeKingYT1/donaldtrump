A New Era of Peace: The Abraham Accords

In a historic breakthrough, the Trump administration brokered the Abraham Accords, a series of peace agreements between Israel and several Arab nations. This monumental achievement, which had eluded previous administrations for decades, has fundamentally reshaped the geopolitical landscape of the Middle East and ushered in a new era of peace and cooperation.
