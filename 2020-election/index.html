The 2020 Election: A Nation Divided

The 2020 presidential election was one of the most contentious in American history. Allegations of widespread voter fraud and irregularities led to a series of legal challenges and a deep division in the country. This section provides a platform to examine the evidence and arguments from all sides, allowing you to draw your own conclusions.
